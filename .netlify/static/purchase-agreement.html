Purchase Agreement

This document outlines the terms and conditions for the purchase of a Cane Corso puppy from CCR Kennels. Please read all terms carefully before placing a deposit.

💰
$500 Deposit
Required to reserve your puppy. Payments accepted until 8 weeks of age.
🤝
Signed at Deposit
This agreement is signed at the time of your deposit transaction.
📄
Health Guarantee
A separate Health Guarantee Contract is signed at the time of pickup.
⚠️
Important Notice

Please ensure you are fully prepared for the time commitment, training, and financial responsibilities associated with a large, dominant breed puppy before placing a deposit. This breed is not suitable for novice dog owners and should be socialized with people, young children, and other dogs regularly as a puppy and throughout adulthood.

TERMS & CONDITIONS

1
Prospective Family Evaluation

CCR Kennels conducts interviews with each prospective family to ensure they are a suitable match for a Cane Corso puppy and understand the commitment involved. This may include completing a questionnaire or adoption application.

2
Time Commitment & Responsibilities

Purchasers acknowledge the significant time, financial, and emotional investment required for a Cane Corso. A stable and caring environment is crucial for a happy and well-adjusted dog. We prioritize placing our puppies in the best possible homes.

3
Training & Socialization

Buyers are encouraged to enroll their puppy in obedience classes and provide proper socialization opportunities. This contributes to a well-behaved and socially competent dog.

4
Buyer Responsibility

The BUYER is responsible for the care, training, and socialization of the puppy. This includes adhering to appropriate training practices and actively addressing any behavioral concerns.

5
Temperament & Match

CCR Kennels will not place a puppy with a family if we believe the temperament of either the puppy or the family is not a suitable match.

6
Building a Relationship

CCR Kennels strives to build long-lasting relationships with all our new families. We consider our puppies as part of our family and want to see them thrive in a loving home.

7
Buyer Commitment

The BUYER confirms that they have adequately prepared themselves and their home for this specific breed. The BUYER is committed to providing the ongoing training and care necessary for the puppy's well-being. Under no circumstances shall the BUYER abandon, sell, surrender to a shelter, or terminate the life of the puppy or dog due to issues related to temperament, size, or color.

8
Return Policy

Should the BUYER find themselves unable to care for the puppy or dog, they agree to return the animal to the breeder at their own expense without any financial restitution.

9
No Guarantees on Traits

CCR Kennels of Southern Illinois does not guarantee the temperament, size, color, or show potential of the puppy.

10
Deposit & Payment

A monetary deposit of $500 is required to reserve a puppy. The BUYER may make payments until the puppies reach 8 weeks of age. The final payment is due at that time, and all shipping or pick-up arrangements must be completed and paid in full. Acceptable payment methods include cash, CashApp, or Venmo.

11
Signing of Agreement

Signing of this Purchase Agreement is conducted at the time of deposit transaction. The Health Guarantee Contract will be signed separately at the time of pickup.

12
Extended Stay

Puppies held beyond the scheduled pick-up date may incur an extended stay fee, or may be offered to another prospective family at our discretion, rendering the original contract null and void.

13
Non-Refundable Deposit

The deposit is non-refundable unless there is a health issue concerning the puppy. In such cases, the BUYER may transfer their deposit and any payments made toward another available puppy.

14
Agreement to All Terms

By placing a deposit on a puppy, the BUYER agrees to all terms and conditions outlined in this Purchase Agreement and the kennel's sales policy. There are no exceptions to these terms.

15
Breach of Agreement

Any civil action will be held at the Marion County Courthouse, District 5, Circuit 4th — 100 E. Main Street, Salem, IL 62881.
