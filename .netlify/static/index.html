Our Story

Welcome to CCR Kennels

Veteran-owned and family-operated, we're dedicated to raising exceptional Italian Cane Corsos with love, care, and commitment to the breed.

The Home of Happy Corsos

At CCR Kennels, located in beautiful Southern Illinois, we are passionate about breeding and raising healthy, well-tempered Italian Cane Corsos. As a veteran-owned business, we bring discipline, dedication, and integrity to everything we do.

Our dogs are raised as family members, socialized from birth, and given the best nutrition and veterinary care. We carefully select our breeding pairs for health, temperament, and adherence to breed standards.

Every puppy that leaves our home is AKC registered, DNA health tested, fully vaccinated, and comes with our comprehensive 2-year health guarantee. We're committed to supporting our puppy families for life.

What We Offer

Our Services

Every CCR Kennels puppy comes with comprehensive care and support to ensure a healthy, happy start to life.

AKC Registration

All our puppies come with full AKC registration papers, documented pedigree, and champion bloodline certification.

DNA Health Testing

Comprehensive genetic health screening on all breeding dogs to ensure your puppy is free from hereditary conditions.

Veterinary Care

Complete vet checks, age-appropriate vaccinations, and deworming protocols before your puppy comes home.

2-Year Health Guarantee

Our comprehensive health warranty covers genetic conditions for two full years, giving you peace of mind.

Dew Claws & Tail Docking

Professional dew claw removal and tail docking performed by our veterinarian at the appropriate age.

Lifelong Breeder Support

We're here for you and your Corso for life. Training tips, health advice, and ongoing guidance whenever you need it.

Nationwide Shipping

Safe, comfortable puppy transport to anywhere in the United States with experienced pet shipping services.

Puppy Care Package

Each puppy goes home with a gift bag including food, toys, blanket with littermate scent, and care instructions.

How It Works

The Adoption Process

We make the process straightforward and transparent from start to go-home day. Here's exactly what to expect.

01

Submit Application

Download and complete our puppy application. Tell us about yourself, your home, and your experience with dogs. This helps us ensure our puppies go to the right families.

Download Application
02

Application Review

We carefully review every application within 48 hours. We may reach out with additional questions. Our goal is to find the perfect match — for you and for the puppy.

03

Approval & Deposit

Once approved, a $500.00 non-refundable security deposit reserves your spot. No payment is accepted before approval. We accept payment via Zelle, Venmo, or cash.

Download Deposit Form
04

Puppy Updates

From birth through go-home day, we send regular photos and videos of your puppy's growth and development. You'll watch your pup grow week by week.

05

Purchase Agreement

Before go-home day, we finalize your Purchase Agreement & Health Guarantee. This outlines our 2-year health warranty, care requirements, and responsibilities on both sides.

Preview Agreement
06

Go-Home Day

At 8 weeks old your puppy is ready! Pick up in person at our home in Centralia, IL — always our first choice — or we'll help coordinate transport. Your puppy goes home with a full care package and lifetime breeder support. Please note: the remaining balance of the total purchase price is due in full prior to the puppy leaving our care. All funds must be fully cleared and confirmed by the financial institution before any puppy is released — no exceptions.

Every CCR Kennels puppy comes with: AKC registration papers, DNA health testing documentation, veterinary health certificate, age-appropriate vaccinations and deworming, dew claws removed, tail docked, 2-year health guarantee, pedigree and AKC packet, puppy care guide, and a gift bag with food, toys, and a blanket with littermate scent.
To reserve a puppy, contact us via phone, email, or our contact form. We'll discuss available puppies or upcoming litters, answer your questions, and if it's a good match, we'll send you our puppy application. Once approved, a $500.00 non-refundable security deposit secures your puppy. No payment is accepted until your application has been reviewed and approved. We provide regular updates with photos and videos until pickup or delivery.

While we don't arrange shipping directly, we are happy to help guide you through the process of setting up transport for your new puppy. All coordination, scheduling, and payment for shipping is the sole responsibility of the buyer. All our breeding dogs undergo comprehensive DNA genetic health testing through Embark or similar certified laboratories. We screen for breed-specific conditions including hip dysplasia, elbow dysplasia, cardiac issues, and genetic disorders. Health testing results are available upon request. We provide a comprehensive 2-year health guarantee covering genetic/hereditary conditions.
